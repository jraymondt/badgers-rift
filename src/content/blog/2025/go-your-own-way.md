## These are the days of our Internets

I am Chuck Aside, and this is the rest of my life in small leaflet sized parcels, beamed over the radio, directly into your spare spatula drawer.

I have just been reflecting at myself, then back once again, with the ill behavior. How damaged are we?
Also, give some credit where credit is due, I got a little prickly recently watching The Stranger Things season three and four.
They really went to town on the cheesy Eighties shit, then I realised the Eighties was ALL over the top cheesy shit... this is an accurate retelling of the time I was alive in.
It was the sugared up then dipped in cocaine bastard step-child of the Sixties and Seventies, just like a used ham in an Xmas punch-up.

The 80's and the 90's were fine, a product of mass consumerism with a healthy bump of cocaine to drive it into... overdrive, I think overdrive may be a product of the Eighties too! <a href="https://music.youtube.com/watch?v=ix5KGdj39Ek&si=A60z0CcdN4WyinqI" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">OverDrive¬Æ</a>
